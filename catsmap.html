🐢Slow down first
Aim for 50% of your normal speed. Your brain needs to build a new spatial map. Speed comes back naturally — errors trained in don't.
🪶Don't bottom out
Mechanical keys actuate before the floor. Train lighter keypresses — reduces fatigue and builds better habits from day one.
📦Box the old keyboard
Switching back resets your learning curve instantly. 72 hours of full immersion is the minimum to start locking in new muscle memory.
🔄Remap problem keys
VIA and QMK let you move any key to where your fingers expect it. If you keep hitting the wrong spot, move the key — not your finger.
💻Developer tips
Bracket pairs, semicolons, and your layer keys are your highest-value targets. Rebuild those before worrying about typing speed.
⏱️10 min daily beats 1 hour
Short focused drills on your problem keys beat marathon frustrated sessions. Cat Attack mode is great for this — pressure without consequence.
